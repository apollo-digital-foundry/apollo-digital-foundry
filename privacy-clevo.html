Clevo

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2025

Introduction

Clevo ("we", "us", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our habit tracking and management application.

Information We Collect

Clevo collects and stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Habit tracking data (names, start dates, progress)
  • Journal entries and personal reflections
  • Urge logs and trigger information
  • Daily cost and savings estimates you provide
  • App preferences and notification settings
  • Milestone and achievement progress

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Health data from Apple HealthKit
  • Browsing history or app usage outside Clevo

How We Store Your Data

All data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage mechanisms. Your data remains under your control at all times.

Local Storage:

  • Data is encrypted using iOS device encryption
  • No external servers or databases are used
  • Data persists only on your device

iCloud Sync (Optional):

  • If enabled, data syncs via Apple's iCloud service
  • iCloud data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Sync can be disabled at any time in Settings

Data Sharing

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties for any purpose.

  • No advertising networks or ad tracking
  • No analytics services that identify users
  • No data brokers or marketing partners
  • No social media integrations that share data

Your Rights and Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your data within the app at any time
  • Export: Download your data using the Export feature
  • Delete: Remove individual entries or all data
  • Portability: Export data in a readable format

To delete all app data, you can use "Clear All Data" in Settings or uninstall the app.

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you choose to delete it. We do not have access to your data and therefore cannot delete it on your behalf. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data. If iCloud Sync is enabled, you must also delete data from iCloud separately.

Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Data stored using iOS secure storage APIs
  • Device-level encryption protects stored data
  • No transmission of data to external servers
  • No account credentials stored

Children's Privacy

Clevo is not directed at children under 13 years of age (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can address the situation.

International Users

Clevo stores all data locally on your device. If you enable iCloud Sync, your data may be transferred to and stored on servers in countries where Apple operates data centers, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since we do not collect, sell, or share personal information, most CCPA provisions do not apply. You may still contact us with any privacy-related questions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected in the app with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
