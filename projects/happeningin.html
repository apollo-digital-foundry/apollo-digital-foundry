§ 01 / OVERVIEW

HappeningIn is a local discovery product for people who want to know what's happening in Seattle without fighting social feeds, calendar spam, or buried venue pages.

The first release is intentionally simple: a chronological city feed, clean event detail pages, and direct links back to official sources.

§ 02 / CAPABILITIES

  • Curated Seattle events
  • Chronological feed
  • Event detail pages
  • Official source links
  • Local AI extraction
  • Human-grade curation
  • No accounts
  • No algorithm
