§ 01 / OVERVIEW

SendEstimates is a mobile-first quoting tool for plumbers, electricians, landscapers, and other trade pros. Build a clean, branded estimate from a job site, send it for e-signature, and follow up automatically — without a laptop in sight.

Replaces back-of-the-truck handwritten quotes and three-day desktop turnarounds with a two-minute mobile flow.

§ 02 / CAPABILITIES

  • Mobile quote builder
  • Reusable line items
  • Auto-branded PDFs
  • E-signatures
  • Auto follow-ups
  • Stripe deposits
  • Client portal
  • CSV / QuickBooks export
