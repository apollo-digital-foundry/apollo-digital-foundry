§ 01 / OVERVIEW

Clevo is a habit-breaking companion built for the moment of urge. It tracks the streaks that matter, surfaces the patterns behind your triggers, and keeps motivation in reach when willpower runs thin.

Designed for nicotine, vaping, alcohol, gambling and other compulsive habits — Clevo turns recovery into a visible, measurable, daily practice.
